Tab

The screenshot of tabs

A tab is a rectangular form "link", that is in the top left side of the navigator in RixxyPlayer.

Currently, there are 3 tabs.

What can tabs do?

Tabs in Scratch

Tabs do nothing special, you can just switch them one to another. Like in Scratch.

If you press a tab named "Docs", you will get linked to the homepage of the Docs.

Trivia

  • In the April Fools, the "Docs" tab links to a Bit.ly link.
  • Tabs were inspired by the tabs in Scratch.
  • 2 tab-looking things in the right are actually not tabs. Their place they are is named as The Uploader.
